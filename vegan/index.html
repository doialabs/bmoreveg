Best Vegan Restaurants in Baltimore

Baltimore's vegan dining scene has grown significantly, with options ranging from upscale globally inspired cuisine to authentic South Indian street food. These are the spots where plant-based diners can order confidently — whether it's a fully vegan restaurant or a place with extensive, dedicated vegan options that go well beyond a token salad.
