Fells Point

Veg-friendly dining in Fells Point

Fells Point's cobblestone streets are home to some of Baltimore's most distinctive veg dining. Little Donna's blends Polish and Italian cuisines in a cozy living-room setting, Ekiben operates a popular location here with generous Asian fusion bowls, and just up the hill into Butchers Hill, La Barrita Restobar serves warm Argentinian empanadas and grilled provoleta. It's a neighborhood where every spot has real personality.
