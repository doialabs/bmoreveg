Reisterstown

Veg-friendly dining in Reisterstown

Reisterstown rewards the adventurous diner with Little Georgia, an authentic Georgian restaurant run by a former postal worker who followed a culinary calling. The khachapuri (boat-shaped cheese bread) and khinkali dumplings are expertly crafted, and the wine list features 450 Georgian varietals spanning 10,000 years of winemaking tradition.
