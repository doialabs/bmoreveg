Canton

Veg-friendly dining in Canton

Canton's waterfront neighborhood offers a strong mix of veg-friendly options. Cafe Dear Leon's Japanese-inspired pastries draw lines every morning, Love Pomelo serves house-made Italian pasta on Canton Square, and Di Pasquale's Italian Marketplace has been making fresh mozzarella and vegetarian lasagna since 1914. From brunch pastries to family-run Italian, Canton covers all the bases.
