Hampden

Veg-friendly dining in Hampden

Hampden is one of Baltimore's most walkable neighborhoods for veg-friendly dining. The Avenue (36th Street) and surrounding blocks are home to Artifact Coffee's spacious warehouse roastery, Ekiben's bold Taiwanese-Ethiopian fusion bowls, and True Chesapeake Oyster Co. inside the historic Whitehall Mill. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee or sitting down for a full seafood dinner, Hampden delivers character and quality.
