Best Veg-Friendly Brunch in Baltimore

Baltimore's brunch scene is strong for veg diners, from cult-favorite bakeries that sell out before noon to spacious cafes perfect for a lazy weekend morning. These spots all serve standout food during morning and early afternoon hours — pastries, coffee, and light meals that make getting up on a Saturday actually worthwhile.
