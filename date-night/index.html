Best Veg-Friendly Date Night Restaurants in Baltimore

Looking for a veg-friendly restaurant with atmosphere? Baltimore has plenty of spots where the setting matches the food. From candlelit Italian to ornate Turkish, farm-to-table Indian to waterfront seafood, these are the restaurants worth dressing up for — all with strong vegetarian and plant-forward options.
