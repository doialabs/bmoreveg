Veg-friendly dining in Charles Village

Charles Village's university-adjacent dining scene includes Peter Chang, a Sichuan restaurant serving fast, outstanding vegetable dishes at wallet-friendly prices. The eggplant preparation alone is worth the visit — silky, properly oiled, with authentic Sichuan numbing heat. No frills, no fuss, just excellent food.
